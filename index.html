Shoud You Attend?

Chances are unless you are very lucky you will go thru many different relationships before you find your special someone. Who knew in the matter of choosing your juniors, you would hardly have a choice. Yet here we are, loving and respecting you, learning from you, but the lord knows we can't match up to the work you guys have done. Passing onto us the wisdom you procured over the years, you gave us the flame that burned us to push harder, understand who we were, and evolve into who we became. Yes we just described a butterfly but well we believe we were quite similar. So from a messed up batch to the one who miraculously had everything figured out, WE NEED A FINAL TALK!!

Sometimes we think about the goodbyes in order in the next few months and everything up until now seems worthless, insignificant but remembering the laughter, the smiles, bitching, hangouts, heart to hearts, drunken conversations and treats, all of the struggle seems to not matter anymore. The tears seem to be for the people we were when we met you, and the people with whom we grew into what we are today. Please make it, if only to see the people you morphed us to be.

invitation

Four lovely and momentous years filled with genuine smiles, moving drunken speeches, coherent and incoherent heart to heart conversations and beautiful displays of camaraderie have finally reached its culmination. It seems like only yesterday when you had stepped into college bright faced and dewy eyed and yet four years seem to have gone by in a flash and the time to say goodbye has finally arrived.

We come to you bearing not just an invitation to share our last good-byes lovely seniors, but bearing an invitation to come relive your best memories, to swap stories, to giggle over your old inside jokes and to share the warm mirth that enkindles the passion of your souls.
